## Problem Statement

Car rental businesses have no reliable digital storefront to showcase their fleet, manage availability visibility, and convert browsing visitors into confirmed bookings — forcing customers to rely on phone calls, walk-ins, or generic booking platforms that offer no brand control.

## Objective

Build a car rental booking website that lets customers search available vehicles by location, date, and car type; browse the fleet with detailed specs and pricing; and complete a booking end-to-end — while giving the business a professional web presence that builds trust and drives direct reservations.

## Key Features

- **Vehicle Search** — Search available vehicles by location, date, and car type
- **Fleet Browsing** — Browse the fleet with detailed specs and pricing
- **End-to-End Booking** — Complete a booking from search to confirmation
- **Professional Web Presence** — A branded digital storefront that builds trust and drives direct reservations
- **Availability Visibility** — Real-time visibility into vehicle availability

## Core Problem

Customers currently rely on:
- Phone calls
- Walk-ins
- Generic booking platforms with no brand control

## Desired Outcome

A professional, branded car rental booking website that replaces fragmented offline/generic channels with a seamless digital experience — driving direct reservations and giving the business full control over its online presence.
